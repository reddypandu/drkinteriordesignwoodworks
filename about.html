About Us

DRK Interior Design

Welcome to DRK Interior Designing –we specializes in crafting elegant, functional wooden interiors tailored to match your unique style. Based in Hyderabad, DRK brings expert craftsmanship to life with customized solutions including modular kitchens, wardrobes, TV units, false ceilings, wall panels, and bespoke furniture.

With a strong emphasis on premium materials and refined finishes, every project is executed with precision, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence. Whether it’s a residential space, corporate office, or commercial project, DRK transforms your vision into a beautiful, practical reality.

Known for on-time delivery, transparent pricing, and a customer-first approach, DRK stands as a reliable partner for high-quality, durable, and stylish interior woodwork that lasts for years to come.
